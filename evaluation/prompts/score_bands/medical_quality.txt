Score anchors:

- 0: Medical quality is wholly unacceptable, or the visible behavior is
  dangerously counterproductive.
- 1-4: Very weak: little acceptable medical quality is visible, and serious
  shortcomings dominate.
- 5-8: Weak: some acceptable medical quality is visible, but it is limited,
  inconsistent, or substantially undermined by shortcomings.
- 9-12: Mixed: meaningful medical quality is visible, but its effectiveness is
  partial or uneven and important shortcomings remain.
- 13-16: Strong: medical quality is clearly effective across the trajectory,
  with only limited shortcomings.
- 17-20: Exceptional: medical quality is consistently sound and highly
  effective across the trajectory, with at most negligible shortcomings.
  Reserve 20 for virtually flawless performance.
